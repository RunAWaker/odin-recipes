Pasta

Description

Pasta is a tradional Italian dish made from wheat and water, often served with sauces.

It comes in many shapes and sizes, like spagetti, penne and fusilli

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spagehtti, penne, fusilli etc.)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or marinara
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. Add a pinch of salt
  3. Cook the pasta until al dente
  4. Drain the pasta and save a litle pasta water
  5. Heat sauce in a pan
  6. Mix pasta with the sauce (add pasta if needed)
  7. Serve with cheese, herb or toppings
