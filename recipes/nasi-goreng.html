Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is a popular indonesian fried rice dish, usually cooked with vegetables, eggs and a savory soybased sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Garlic
  • Shallots or onions
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili or chili paste
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saute garlic and shallots until fragrant
  2. Add protein and cook until done
  3. Add vegetable and stir-fry briefly
  4. Push ingredients aside, scramble eggs in the same pan
  5. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing everything well
  6. Serve hot, garnished with green onions or fried shallots
