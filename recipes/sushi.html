Sushi

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, often topped with a raw fish, seafood or vegetables. It comes in many varieties, like nigiri, maki rolls and sashimi and its usually server with soy sauce, wasabi and pickled ginger.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Nori sheets
  • Raw fish
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Cook and season the sushi rice with rice vinegar, sugar and salt
  2. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat
  3. Spread the rice evenly over the nori
  4. Add fillings like fish and vegetables
  5. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat
  6. Slice ino pieces and serve with soy sauce, wasabi and pickled ginger
